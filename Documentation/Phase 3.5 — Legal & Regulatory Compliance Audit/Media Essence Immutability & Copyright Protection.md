## 3.5-1 Media Essence Immutability & Copyright Protection

### Status
Compliant (Strong)

### Legal Basis
- Copyright non-derogation
- Moral rights protection
- Chain-of-custody integrity

### Design Guarantees
- Media essence is read-only by default
- Any override requires explicit user authorization
- All overrides are fully logged and auditable
- No hidden stream modification is permitted

### Compliance Outcome
Prevents unauthorized content alteration, accidental derivative creation,
and silent quality modification, satisfying professional copyright
and archival integrity requirements.

### Action Required
None
