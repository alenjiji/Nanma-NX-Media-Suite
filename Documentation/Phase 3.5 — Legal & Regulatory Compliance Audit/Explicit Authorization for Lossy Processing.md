## 3.5-2 Explicit Authorization for Lossy Processing

### Status
Compliant

### Legal Basis
- Informed consent
- Contractual disclosure obligations

### Design Guarantees
- Lossy operations are disabled by default
- Explicit user authorization is mandatory
- Quality impact is disclosed before execution
- All lossy actions are logged and auditable

### Compliance Outcome
Eliminates silent quality degradation and satisfies professional disclosure
requirements in broadcast and post-production contracts.

### Action Required
None
