## 3.5-3 Deterministic Execution & Legal Reproducibility

### Status
Compliant

### Legal Basis
- Audit reproducibility
- Forensic verification
- Broadcast dispute defense

### Design Guarantees
- Bit-identical output for identical inputs
- Deterministic scheduling and processing
- Seeded randomness where required
- No dependency on system time or hardware variance

### Compliance Outcome
Enables exact reproduction of results for audits,
legal disputes, and compliance verification.

### Action Required
None
