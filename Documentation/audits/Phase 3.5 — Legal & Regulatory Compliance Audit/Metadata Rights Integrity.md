## 3.5-5 Metadata Rights Integrity

### Status
Compliant

### Legal Basis
- Rights metadata accuracy
- Licensing integrity
- Copyright management

### Design Guarantees
- No automatic metadata inference
- Explicit authorization for all metadata changes
- Rights metadata handled as protected category
- Full metadata diff reporting

### Compliance Outcome
Reduces risk of rights misrepresentation
and licensing disputes.

### Action Required
None
