## 3.5-2 Media Essence Immutability & Chain of Custody

### Status
Compliant

### Forensic Basis

- Explicit authorization required for overrides
- All overrides are logged and auditable

Prevents silent modification and preserves
verifiable chain-of-custody for media assets.
