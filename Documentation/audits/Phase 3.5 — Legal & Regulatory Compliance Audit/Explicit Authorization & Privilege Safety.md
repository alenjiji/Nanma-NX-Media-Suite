## 3.5-3 Explicit Authorization & Privilege Safety

### Status
Compliant

### Security Basis
- Privilege escalation prevention
- Insider misuse containment

### Design Guarantees
- High-risk operations disabled by default
- Explicit authorization required
- Full parameter disclosure and logging

### Security Outcome
Prevents silent misuse, accidental damage,
and unauthorized quality degradation.

### Action Required
None
